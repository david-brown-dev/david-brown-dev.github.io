My EDA Learning Plan

My draft learning plan for the EDA Web Development course. It sets out my objectives for learning, current strengths and weaknesses, commitment to the process and expectations on facilitators.

My Learning Objectives

My learning goals at EDA are primarily technical with a focus on developing my capabilities in the technical aspects of Web Development. I also want to build my ability to work in an Agile team environment. My career goal is to become a web developer long-term or at some point transition to a product management position. The capabilities I want to focus on during my time at EDA are:

  • JavaScript front-end development interacting with DOM elements and managing front-end communication with APIs
  • JavaScript back-end development, data transformation and building APIs with node.js etc
  • CSS manipulation, planning and using frameworks such as bootstrap
  • HTML usage and best practice for accessible websites
  • Agile based, group working situations

My Key Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths:

  • 18 years of IT experience for contextualisation
  • Strong questioning and communication skills, including explaining complex concepts
  • Ability to analyse and solve complex problems
  • Business acumen

Weaknesses:

  • Limited development knowledge and experience with HTML, CSS and JS
  • Poor understanding of development ecosystem
  • No recent experience of working in team environments and I have never had to work so closely with others before. I may struggle with patience and very different personalities than Iâ€™m used to

My Commitment

I am 100% dedicated to this course and making the most of my opportunity. Each week of foundations I will ensure that I clearly understand my learning objectives and complete the stretch activities. I will also use any leftover time to deepen my learning in the cover concepts. I will dedicate at minimum 40 hours per week to foundations and boot camp with the boot camp hours being on campus.

When working with others in pair or team environments, I will ensure I honour my commits of time and work to my team. I will commit to being a reliable and patient learning partner, respecting others learning journeys and scheduling challenges.

Finally, I will manage my workload, stress levels and productivity by ensuring that I reach out to facilitators when I need help with something I can solve myself. I will do this either face to face or via slack.

My Expectations of the Facilitation Team

  • To give good guidance on learning resources that I can use to improve my understanding of concepts
  • Facilitate active classroom environments that enable learning from teachers, peers and practical activities
  • To be responsive if my pace of learning is faster than other class members
  • Be clear and accurate with communication
  • Have a high degree of integrity, do what you commit to

My Scheduling Challenges

There are no ongoing reasons that I would not be able to dedicate my full attention and attendance however should one arise, I will promptly discuss with my facilitator.

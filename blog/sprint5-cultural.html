Reflections on Foundations Core Skills

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

Not sure if I can name three but the one that comes to mind was during the Identity and values exploration exercise, I was able to see just how important self-awareness is to me and put a name to it for the first time as a value that as important to me. The other one I would say is during the looping and listening activity, just how important to me empathy is.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Honestly, just difficult and rare listening is for people!

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Putting together my learning plan as it’s not a document I have ever written before and I really wasn’t sure how to frame it.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Because your core focus is to train for employment and IT employers are desire programmers with good people skills over and above technical skills.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer

No not at all. Firstly there wasn’t that much time dedicated to it so the work in core was not onerous. Also the core skills genuinely are desirable among employers. Finally I think they will be critical for a bootcamp scenario where people are working so closely together using pair programming. If we hadn’t covered these, the bootcamp may be much less productive.
